The Effects of Global Warming



There have been many significant and severe consequences due to the careless nature of humanity, "Global Warming" as we know it is very significant and can cause unreversible damage if left to continue growing to destroy habitats and extinguish many species of animals. There are countless effects of global warming and everyone should be alarmed about the harms that come from it so we can make a stand and make a positive impact.


Examples of such effects are:


  1. Hotter temperatures

  2. More severe storms

  3. Increased drought

  4. A warming, rising ocean

  5. Loss of species and biodiversity
